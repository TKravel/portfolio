Hi, I'm Tim Kravel!

As a developer fueled by a passion for problem-solving and innovation, I currently thrive in a dynamic role at a local startup. Specializing as a full-stack developer, I harness the power of the LEMP stack to craft web applications that push boundaries.

My journey into programming commenced unexpectedly in 2012, amidst the whirring machinery of a CNC machine shop. Here, I wrote my first program using the language of G-code, laying the foundation for my future endeavors. Yet, my curiosity knew no bounds, propelling me into the diverse realms of programming. It was in the boundless universe of web development that I discovered my true calling, igniting a fervent love for crafting digital experiences that resonate.

When the doors of the CNC shop closed in 2020, I seized the moment as an opportunity for transformation. With unwavering determination I fully immersed myself in the realm of web development. It was a leap of faith driven by my insatiable passion for programming and an insatiable thirst for new challenges.

In 2022, I made my professional debut in web development, and since then, I've never looked back. Beyond the lines of code, you'll often find me embracing life's adventures with my son by my side. Whether we're exploring sun-drenched parks, reveling in the wonders of nature, or casting lines into tranquil waters, every moment is an opportunity for discovery and delight.
